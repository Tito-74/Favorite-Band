Elani Band

Elani are a Kenyan African pop group from Nairobi. It consists three members; Wambui Ngugi, Brian Chweya and Maureen Kunga. The trio released their debut single"Jana Usiku" in November 2013 that became a great success, creating a pavement on their subsequent singles. In 2014, the trio released numerous singles in the making of their debut album Barua ya Dunia.

Members of the Band

Maureen Kunga

She was born in lang’ata and schooled in nairobi and later attend Kianda High School before being admitted to University of Nairobi where she graduated with a degree in Law.

Brian Chweya

He wa born in south C, and attended my primary school at Moi Educational Centre, before joining Upper Hill school for his secondary education.   He later joined University of Nairobi for his Law degree and was later admitted to the bar and now he is an advocate.

Wambui Ngugi

She was born and raised in the city from a small family of two girls and she is the youngest . She schooled in nairobi and manage join University of Nairobi and graduated with a BSc in Actuarial Science and is very passionate in her christian belief.
